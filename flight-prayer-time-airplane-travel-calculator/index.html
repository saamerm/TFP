Flight prayer time airplane travel Calculator

Enter flight departure and arrival time, along with the prayer times at origin and destination to get the time of prayer

Simply get the prayer times online (you can use islamicfinder.com) and the flight times from your boarding pass, and input them using the time zone they belong. If you are fasting, you can use this to find the time of sunset

Notes -
  • The problem is challenging because the prayer time changes as the time/distance also changes!
  • Currently this tool loops through each minute of the flight and checking when the time and prayer time at the location match (or when time is safely higher by 1 minute)
  • For prayer times, dont use values that are lower than the flight time, or higher than the arrival time.
  • Dev Note: To increase accuracy to the second, you just have to reduce the step counter of the for-loop
  • If you have any feedback, comments or questions, feel free to get in touch with me on LinkedIn or Twitter
How to use this if you are flying from London, UK to Sylhet, Bangladesh-
  • Your departure time is 6 20 PM August 19 UK time. Flight Time Departure = "19 Aug 2023 18:20"
  • Your arrival time is 9 20 AM August 20 Bangladesh time. Flight Time Arrival = "20 Aug 2023 09:20"
  • The maghrib time in London, UK is 8:21 p.m UK time. Prayer Time Destination = "19 Aug 2023 20:21"
  • The maghrib time in Sylhet, Bangladesh is 6:24 p.m Bangladesh time. Prayer Time Origin = "19 Aug 2023 18:24"
  • Pressing calculate shows that you have to pray at "Sat Aug 19 2023 20:08:00" based on the origin time zone
